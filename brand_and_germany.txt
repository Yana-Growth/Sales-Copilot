Brand Platform & Positioning
Why we're here
This presentation brings together how Tumodo thinks, speaks and acts as a brand. It explains the ideas that guide our product, our communication and the way we create a consistent experience for every client. A clear brand platform keeps everyone aligned from product and design to sales and support so Tumodo sounds and feels the same wherever clients interact with us.

Brand Essence
Tumodo is a simple and convenient platform for managing business travel. One place to book, manage and close every trip — quickly and easily. Clear processes and transparent pricing make business travel predictable. A reliable, helpful ally that supports your team at every step.

Why companies need business travel
Companies send employees on business trips for one core reason: to drive growth. People travel to achieve KPIs, explore new markets, meet partners and unlock opportunities for their business. A corporate trip is never just flights and hotels. It's a chance to move the company forward, open markets, create new value, build relationships that matter and establish long-term trust. Growth happens when people meet, talk and make decisions together.

Why travel management matters
Growth needs structure: When travel is organised through a seamless, clear process, the business no longer wastes money or employee time.
Predictability enables planning: A structured travel flow keeps budgets, approvals and reporting consistent, so companies can plan ahead confidently.
Controlled travel means a controlled budget: With transparent prices, a smart travel policy and real-time analytics, companies gain full visibility and stay firmly within budget.

Market Challenges (Pain Points)
Booking takes too long: A trip that should take 5 minutes ends up taking hours. Manual data entry leads to mistakes.
No proper process for closing trips: Missing boarding passes, fines, and no integration with accounting/HRIS.
Ineffective Travel Policy: Rules are unclear or scattered in static PDF files.
Difficult Reporting: Collecting data takes too much time; paper documents get lost and emails get buried.
Outdated Tools: Businesses are held back by slow service and fragmented processes.

What sets Tumodo apart
End-to-end process ownership: Connects everything into one continuous flow, from booking to analytics.
Process-first, not feature-first: Redesigns the logic of business travel from the ground up to automate routine work.
Predictability as a strategic advantage: Creates reliable outcomes in spend, compliance, and decision-making.
Personalisation without configuration pain: Adapts to how companies already work; approval flows mirror actual structures.
Human-centred technology: Combines technology with real human care and 24/7 support.

Our Principles
Simplicity & ease: We remove anything that complicates business travel. We choose clarity over complexity.
Transparency & trust: We show full pricing without hidden conditions. We explain where savings come from.
Innovation: We don't just digitise processes, we rethink how travel should work.
Personalisation: Tumodo adapts to your business rules and habits.
Warmth & empathy: We respond with care, not templates. Specialists are available before, during and after every trip.

Product & Service Details (Germany)
The Solution
An all-in-one solution that eliminates outdated manual processes by digitising approvals and integrating with finance systems. Managers can control all processes from booking to payments.

Core Services
Flights: 500+ airlines worldwide.
Stays: 3M+ accommodation options.
Trains: Rail across Europe and Asia.
Other services: Car transfers in 90+ countries.

Key Features
Built-in Travel Policy: Only policy-compliant options are shown during search.
Flexible Workflows: Customise approval schemes from one-step to multi-level chains.
Employee Tracker: View traveller location and status in real-time.
Smart Reporting: Executive dashboards for spend and compliance; ESG-ready reporting for carbon footprint tracking.
Mobile App: Tickets, vouchers, and itinerary always at hand; scan receipts directly in the app.

Service Models
In-country service: Local content and support for single markets.
Regional service hub: Standardised processes for multi-country clusters.
Global service model: Multinational programmes with consolidated governance.

Commercial Model Options
Transaction-based (online vs assisted fees).
Subscription-based (per user or per month).
Implementation fees for setup and migration.
